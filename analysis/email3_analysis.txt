- No domain verification present.

- Uses a common scenario (parcel delivery) to appear legitimate.

3. Link Analysis:

- Domain is not associated with known delivery services (like Amazon, Flipkart).
- Likely a phishing site to collect user information.

4. Phishing Indicators Identified:
- Suspicious URL domain
- Lack of official branding

5. Risk Classification:
Phishing

7. Potential Impact:

- Exposure to further phishing attacks
